Our Solution: The GreenWorks App

Madagascar GreenWorks is a mobile platform designed to create a self-sustaining ecosystem. It connects people, turns waste into a resource, and empowers communities through technology.

How It Works

A simple, four-part system designed for maximum community impact.

Waste Collection & Sorting Marketplace

This is the core of the app. Households and businesses can request pickups for sorted waste. Registered "Green Collectors" accept these jobs, earn income via mobile money, and ensure waste reaches the right destination.
